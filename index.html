Meet the innovative businesses that have transformed ideas into thriving enterprises through our program

RenewRX

RenewRx is a pregnancy and health app that supports expectant moms with personalized, data-driven coaching in nutrition, lifestyle, and wellness. Working alongside your doctor, it offers tools like glucose monitoring, nutrition tracking, sleep and activity insights, and access to certified coaches to help you build sustainable, healthy habits—before, during, and after pregnancy.

Bring Coffee

Bring Coffee is a craft coffee startup that makes brewing a strong, flavorful cup of coffee as simple as brewing tea. Their innovative solution is perfect for travelers or anyone away from traditional coffee setups. Easy to use, it delivers quality craft-ground coffee with sustainable packaging and convenient portability. This is what makes Bring Coffee Amazing for coffee lovers.

Tap Energy

Tap Energy is a clean tech company that creates electricity from household tap water pressure. Their device converts kinetic energy from flowing water into sustainable power, reducing reliance on traditional energy sources. Easy to install, it lowers energy costs and carbon footprints by integrating directly into existing plumbing systems. This is what makes Tap Energy Amaizing.

Unlocking Athletes' Full Potential

10K Athletics emerged from the Innovation Center with a vision to transform athlete development through an all-in-one platform that connects players, coaches, and parents.

The Challenge

Athletes and coaches struggle with fragmented training tools and limited performance insights. Traditional athletic development lacks personalized, data-driven approaches that help athletes reach their peak potential.

The Innovation

10K Athletics provides personalized training plans, real-time performance tracking, and comprehensive insights that help athletes, coaches, and parents work together. The platform combines sports science with modern technology to optimize athletic development.

Innovation Center Impact

The Innovation Center helped 10K Athletics refine their product-market fit and scale their platform. With mentorship in sports technology and business development, 10K Athletics has grown to serve thousands of athletes nationwide.

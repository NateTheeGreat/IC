Turning Tap Water into Clean Electricity

Tap Energy, our 2023 Innovation Challenge winner, transforms everyday water pressure into clean electricity, proving that innovation can turn the mundane into the extraordinary.

The Challenge

Clean energy solutions often require significant infrastructure or environmental impact. Billions of gallons of water flow through pipes daily with untapped kinetic energy that could power homes and reduce dependence on traditional electricity sources.

The Innovation

Tap Energy developed a revolutionary device that captures the kinetic energy from water pressure in existing plumbing systems. This groundbreaking technology generates clean electricity without any modifications to infrastructure or impact on water flow.

Innovation Center Impact

As the 2023 Innovation Challenge winner, Tap Energy received critical funding and mentorship to advance their technology from prototype to market. The Innovation Center's network helped them connect with industry experts and potential partners in the clean energy sector.

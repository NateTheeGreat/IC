This Year's Businesses

Meet the innovative teams competing in the 2025-2026 Regis Innovation Center Challenge. These visionary entrepreneurs are building the next generation of transformative startups across technology, health, sustainability, and social impact.

Explore Businesses

Connecting food lovers with weekly deals

Hungry is a location-based food and event deals app designed to connect users with weekly restaurant discounts tailored to their tastes. Users can redeem special offers such as Taco Tuesday deals, Thursday tequila BOGO, and Monday margaritas and earn points each time they use an offer. For restaurants, Hungry provides an easy and cost-effective way to promote themselves without needing to run large email campaigns or spend money on traditional marketing.

Building the infrastructure for the EV world

MeanderEV is building a nationwide and international network of monetized Level-2 chargers powered by their app. They're creating a world where "home charging" is available to anyone, not just homeowners. With strong partnerships forming in Puerto Rico, Bermuda, and beyond, MeanderEV is redefining EV charging across the U.S., and ultimately, the world.

Reclaiming sports as a simple way to play

Pickup Play is connecting the pickup sports community in real-time. They're reclaiming sports as a simple way to go play by introducing people with the right groups along with the space to accommodate their game. A player-centric network marketplace driven by users self-organizing games in their neighborhoods while aligning them with the spaces and programs available near them.

Fighting deepfakes with shared memories

realxreal.ai is tackling the $1 trillion global scam crisis with a revolutionary approach to fraud prevention. Instead of detecting fakes, they verify humans through something AI can never access: shared memories. Their trusted contacts app verifies each connection through shared memories you've lived together, creating a secure way to confirm identity before calls, messages, or video chats begin.

View Website

Empowering financial confidence for young people

SEED UP is a gamified financial-learning and investing app built to help young people understand money and investing in a simple, fun, and safe way. Users learn through micro-lessons, complete missions, and practice investing in a virtual market simulator before ever risking real money. Their mission is to empower beginners with financial confidence by combining education, gamification, and hands-on practice.

More than just a workout—it's a sanctuary

Third Space Pilates is a modern wellness Pilates studio designed to be more than just a workout. Unlike traditional fitness studios where clients rush in and out, Third Space creates an intentional environment that blends Pilates, community, and mental wellbeing. Think spa-inspired locker rooms, lounge spaces, and killer Pilates classes creating a "third space"—a place outside of home and work where people feel grounded, supported, and connected.

Fostering friendship and belonging in children

Time to Play is addressing the youth mental health crisis by fostering friendship and belonging in elementary age students. They make playdates easy so that they actually happen. Their AI-powered app takes the mental load off parents by simplifying playdate scheduling and helping them manage their children's social lives with ease, building positive peer experiences that protect against anxiety and depression throughout life.

View Website

Where luxury hospitality meets family life

Tot Friendly Homes is a short-term rental consultancy that partners with property owners to create thoughtfully designed, family-ready stays for guests with children under the age of five. They bridge the gap between luxury hospitality and real family life, where elevated design meets everyday, essential comfort. Each property meets their verified standards for safety, style, and ease—making family travel feel effortless.

Making childcare accessible for every family

Tutio is a platform designed to make childcare more accessible for every family in Colorado. Their platform connects parents to affordable, verified daycare options through an intuitive search experience that filters by price, location, hours, availability, and educational focus. For providers, Tutio offers a simple way to list openings, manage inquiries, and reach more families without expensive marketing.

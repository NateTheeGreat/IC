WHY MENTORSHIP MATTERS

MENTORS ARE GUIDES, CHALLENGERS, CONNECTORS, AND LISTENERS.

The Innovation Challenge pairs teams with mentors because entrepreneurship is a community sport. Mentors help set expectations, provide guidance, and open doors for participants.

All experiences are valuable - whether you're a business faculty member, Regis alumni, or community leader, your expertise shapes the future of these ventures.

TIME COMMITMENT

THREE SATURDAYS IN SPRING 2026

We ask mentors to accompany the founders on three key Saturdays: February 7th, March 7th, and March 28th. These days provide overviews of strategy, marketing, finance, legal, and community resources.

The finals are at 5:30pm on April 17th. Active discussions with mentors help businesses apply this learning so they can grow and succeed.

We put multiple mentors around each team, realizing you might not be able to make all three Saturdays. In between monthly meetings, mentors provide direction through occasional contact.

WHO CAN PARTICIPATE

OPEN TO EVERYONE IN THE REGIS ECOSYSTEM

This competition is open to anyone – the community, alumni, faculty, staff, and students. Each team must have a current Regis student on it.

We want our students to run businesses that anchor their communities. That is why the Regis Innovation Challenge is different in how we mentor our finalists - bringing together diverse perspectives and experiences to create lasting impact.

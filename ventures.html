Innovation is the key to entrepreneurial success

Explore inspiring stories from Regis Innovation Center alumni on ways to build or accelerate successful ventures.

→ More tips and tricks for building successful ventures

RenewRX

Revolutionizing pregnancy care through personalized, data-driven coaching that supports expectant mothers with comprehensive tools for nutrition, lifestyle, and wellness.

Agera

Connects consumers to formal and informal retailers in Africa, bridging the gap between traditional commerce and modern technology across the continent.

10K Athletics

All-in-one platform that connects players, coaches, and parents with personalized training plans and real-time performance insights to unlock athletes' full potential.

Tap Energy

2023 Innovation Challenge winner that transforms everyday water pressure into clean electricity, proving that innovation can turn the mundane into the extraordinary.

Autumn Meadows

Culturally sensitive nursing home that aims to solve the challenge of finding elder care that is sensitive to diverse cultural backgrounds and traditions.

Traveling Ground

Platform that allows users to get great deals for their used bicycles. Now thriving at RiNo, proving that sustainable transportation and smart marketplace technology can create lasting impact.

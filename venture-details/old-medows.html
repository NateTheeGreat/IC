SENIOR CARE

Culturally Sensitive Elder Care

Nursing home that honors diverse cultural traditions and provides dignified care for all communities

Regis Innovation Center | Venture Spotlight
December 2, 2025 • 5 min read

Old Medows is a culturally sensitive nursing home that aims to solve the one problem many people face when they grow old: finding a nursing home that is sensitive to their culture.

The Challenge

As our population becomes increasingly diverse, many families struggle to find elder care facilities that honor and respect their cultural traditions, languages, and customs. Traditional nursing homes often lack the cultural competency needed to provide truly dignified care.

The Innovation

Old Medows has created a compassionate approach to elder care that honors diverse backgrounds. The facility provides culturally appropriate meals, celebrates diverse holidays and traditions, and employs staff who speak multiple languages and understand different cultural perspectives on aging and care.

Care approach includes:

  • Culturally appropriate meal planning and preparation
  • Multilingual staff who speak residents' native languages
  • Celebration of diverse cultural holidays and traditions
  • Respect for different religious and spiritual practices
  • Family-centered care that involves cultural communities

Community Impact

Old Medows residents and their families report significantly higher satisfaction with care that respects their cultural identity. The facility has become a model for how elder care can be both compassionate and culturally aware.

Innovation Center Impact

Through the Innovation Center, Old Medows developed their business model and secured initial funding. The startup has grown from a concept to a thriving facility that demonstrates how elder care can be both compassionate and culturally aware, serving as a model for the future of senior living.

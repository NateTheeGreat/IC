SUSTAINABLE MARKETPLACE

Great Deals for Used Bicycles

Trusted marketplace connecting bicycle sellers with buyers while promoting sustainable transportation

Regis Innovation Center | Venture Spotlight
December 2, 2025 • 5 min read

Traveling Ground is a platform that allows users to get a great deal for their used bicycles and has had a lot of success. Now thriving at RiNo, this startup proves that sustainable transportation and smart marketplace technology can create lasting community impact.

The Challenge

Buying and selling used bicycles traditionally involves uncertainty about value, condition, and fair pricing. Many cyclists upgrade their bikes regularly, but lack a reliable marketplace that ensures quality and fair deals for both buyers and sellers.

The Innovation

Traveling Ground created a trusted platform that connects bicycle sellers with buyers while ensuring quality and fair pricing. The marketplace includes bike inspections, transparent pricing, and community trust features that make buying and selling used bicycles simple and reliable.

Platform features:

  • Professional bike inspections and condition reports
  • Fair market value pricing tools
  • Secure payment and transaction processing
  • Community ratings and reviews
  • Educational resources for bike maintenance and selection

Community Impact

Traveling Ground has facilitated thousands of bicycle transactions, promoting sustainable transportation while building a strong cycling community in Denver. The platform makes cycling more accessible by connecting people with quality used bikes at fair prices.

Innovation Center Impact

From the Innovation Center to success at RiNo, Traveling Ground has demonstrated how a focused marketplace can thrive. The platform has facilitated thousands of bicycle transactions, promoting sustainable transportation while building a strong cycling community in Denver.

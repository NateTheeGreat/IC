How RenewRX is Revolutionizing Pregnancy Care

Personalized, data-driven coaching that supports expectant mothers with comprehensive tools for nutrition, lifestyle, and wellness

Regis Innovation Center | Innovation Challenge Winner 2025
December 2, 2025 • 5 min read

RenewRx is revolutionizing pregnancy care through personalized, data-driven coaching that supports expectant mothers with comprehensive tools for nutrition, lifestyle, and wellness.

The Challenge

Pregnancy care has remained largely unchanged for decades, with expectant mothers often feeling overwhelmed by conflicting advice and limited personalized support. Traditional prenatal care focuses on periodic checkups but lacks continuous, data-driven guidance for daily decisions about nutrition, activity, and wellness.

The Innovation

What sets RenewRX apart is its comprehensive approach to pregnancy wellness. The platform integrates glucose monitoring, nutrition tracking, sleep analysis, and activity insights with access to certified coaches, creating a complete ecosystem of support for maternal health.

Key features include:

  • Glucose monitoring and nutrition tracking
  • Sleep and activity insights
  • Access to certified pregnancy coaches
  • Personalized wellness recommendations
  • Data-driven health insights

Real-World Impact

RenewRX users report improved pregnancy outcomes, reduced anxiety, and better preparedness for motherhood. By working alongside healthcare providers, the platform enhances traditional prenatal care without replacing the crucial doctor-patient relationship.

Innovation Center Impact

RenewRX's journey through the Innovation Center exemplifies how bold ideas can transform into life-changing solutions. From initial concept to Innovation Challenge winner, the startup has leveraged mentorship, funding, and community support to scale their impact in maternal healthcare.

About the innovation Challenge

Empowering the next generation of entrepreneurs and innovators to transform ideas into impactful solutions

OPEN TO THE COMMUNITY

THE ONLY INNOVATION CHALLENGE OPEN TO THE ENTIRE COMMUNITY.

The Regis Innovation Challenge welcomes students, alumni, faculty, and community members from all backgrounds and locations. We've hosted participants from around the world, bringing diverse perspectives and groundbreaking ideas to our stage.

Whether you're a local entrepreneur or an international innovator with a bold vision, you're welcome to join our challenge and bring your ideas to life.

FULLY STUDENT RUN PROGRAMME

EMPOWERING STUDENTS TO LEAD AND INNOVATE.

The Regis Innovation Center is proudly led by students, for students. Our programmes are designed, managed, and executed by passionate student leaders who understand the entrepreneurial journey firsthand.

This student-driven approach creates an authentic, dynamic environment where innovation thrives through peer collaboration, hands-on experience, and real-world problem solving.

Bring Coffee revolutionizes portable craft coffee for the modern traveler

Craft coffee startup makes brewing exceptional coffee as simple as making tea, with sustainability and portability at its core

Regis Innovation Center | Winner 2024
March 2024 • 6 min read

Bring Coffee is a craft coffee startup that emerged from the 2024 Regis Innovation Challenge as the winning venture. Their mission: to make brewing a strong, flavorful cup of coffee as straightforward and convenient as brewing tea—especially for travelers or anyone away from the traditional coffee setup.

As the 2024 winner of the Regis Innovation Challenge, Bring Coffee emphasizes sustainability, portability, and quality, offering craft-ground coffee packaged or designed for on-the-go use. By participating in the competition, they gained mentorship and pitching experience, and secured recognition for moving into a higher-impact phase of development.

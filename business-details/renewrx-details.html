RenewRX revolutionizes pregnancy and wellness with personalized coaching

Innovative health app supports expectant mothers with data-driven coaching in nutrition, lifestyle, and wellness throughout their pregnancy journey

Regis Innovation Center | Winner 2025
March 2025 • 5 min read

RenewRx is a pregnancy and health app that supports expectant moms with personalized, data-driven coaching in nutrition, lifestyle, and wellness. Working alongside your doctor, it offers tools like glucose monitoring, nutrition tracking, sleep and activity insights, and access to certified coaches to help you build sustainable, healthy habits—before, during, and after pregnancy.

As the 2025 winner of the Regis Innovation Center competition, RenewRX emerged as the 2025 winner of the Regis Innovation Center competition, quickly establishing itself as a trusted companion for expectant mothers seeking evidence-based health guidance.

The Challenge

Pregnancy brings countless questions about nutrition, lifestyle choices, and health management. Many expectant mothers struggle to find personalized, reliable guidance that complements their medical care and adapts to their unique needs.

Traditional approaches often provide generic advice that doesn't account for individual health profiles, preferences, or changing needs throughout pregnancy and postpartum recovery.

The Innovation

RenewRX combines cutting-edge technology with evidence-based health science to create a personalized wellness ecosystem for expectant mothers. The platform works in harmony with medical care, never replacing but always enhancing the relationship between patients and their healthcare providers.

Key features include:

  • Glucose monitoring and nutritional tracking tailored to pregnancy needs
  • Sleep and activity insights that adapt to different pregnancy stages
  • Access to certified health coaches specializing in maternal wellness
  • Personalized meal plans and recipes based on dietary preferences and restrictions
  • Real-time health metrics dashboard that integrates with wearable devices
  • Community support features connecting expectant mothers with similar journeys

Making a Difference

RenewRX users report feeling more confident and informed throughout their pregnancy journey. The app's holistic approach helps mothers develop healthy habits that extend beyond pregnancy, benefiting both mother and child long-term.

Healthcare providers praise the platform for empowering their patients with tools that complement clinical care, leading to better health outcomes and more productive medical appointments.

Vision for the Future

RenewRX continues to expand its capabilities, with plans to incorporate AI-driven insights, expanded postpartum support, and partnerships with leading healthcare institutions to make personalized maternal wellness accessible to every expectant mother.

How can technology transform pregnancy care?

RenewRx is revolutionizing pregnancy care with personalized, data-driven coaching. From glucose monitoring to nutrition tracking, this Innovation Center startup is helping expectant mothers build sustainable, healthy habits throughout their journey.

How do you connect consumers to retailers in Africa?

Agera connects consumers to formal and informal retailers in Africa. This innovative platform emerged from the Innovation Center, bridging the gap between traditional commerce and modern technology across the continent.

What if athletes could unlock their full potential?

10K Athletics emerged from the Innovation Center with a vision to transform athlete development. Their all-in-one platform connects players, coaches, and parents with personalized training plans and real-time performance insights.

Can your tap water power your home?

How do you find a nursing home sensitive to your culture?

Autumn Meadows is a culturally sensitive nursing home that aims to solve the one problem many people face when they grow old: finding a nursing home that is sensitive to their culture. This Innovation Center startup is creating a compassionate approach to elder care that honors diverse backgrounds.

Explore Autumn Meadows →

How do you turn used bicycles into great deals?

Traveling Ground is a platform that allows users to get a great deal for their used bicycles and has had a lot of success. Now thriving at RiNo, this Innovation Center startup proves that sustainable transportation and smart marketplace technology can create lasting community impact.
